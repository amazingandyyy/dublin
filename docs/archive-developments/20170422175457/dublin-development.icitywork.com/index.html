Project Description

Multi-phased development comprised of up to 1,995 residential units, up to 200,000 square feet of commercial uses, 35 acres of public parkland, a 12-acre elementary school site and related infrastructure.  The overall project specific plan (Dublin Crossing Specific Plan) was approved in 2013.   Together, Phases 2 and 3 propose a total of 791 units in 12 neighborhoods. Phase 2 is comprised of 508 units on approximately 36.25 acres and Phase 3 is comprised of 283 units on approximately 18.22 acres.  The Planning Commission will hold two study sessions on April 11 and April 25 to review Phases 2 and 3.

The first study session on April 11, 2017 will cover six neighborhoods (Neighborhoods 10-13, 17-18) and the Recreation Center. The second study session on April 25, 2017 will cover six more neighborhoods (Neighborhoods 7-9, 14-16) and modifications to the Landscape Master Plan.

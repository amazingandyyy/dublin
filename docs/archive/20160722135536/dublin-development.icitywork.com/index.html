Project Description

The City received a preliminary application from IKEA to construct a store on a 27.5 acre site at 5144 and 5344 Martinelli Way in March 2016.  The site plan is under review and is still being refined by the Applicant (IKEA).  Changes to the sizes of the proposed buildings and the site layout are expected, and the site plan will be refined by the applicant in the near future.  The preliminary plan submitted to the City can be reviewed by clicking on the thumbnail below.  At this point, the site is proposed to be developed with a nearly 378,000 square feet IKEA store, additional retail/restaurant spaces, and possibly a 75-room hotel.  An environmental analysis of the proposed project has begun.

Community Outreach

Over the course of the next several months, the City will provide multiple opportunities and venues for the community to learn about the project and provide input to City Staff on the desires of the community. The City will also provide opportunities for online engagement.

Open House – June 13, 2016

The City’s first community meeting/open house about this project took place on Monday, June 13, 2016 at 6:30 pm at the Dublin Civic Center. The meeting was attended by 45 community members who provided their input and thoughts regarding the project proposal. The informational handouts and feedback form from this meeting are available to review. The feedback form with your comments about the project can be emailed to: kristi.bascom@dublin.ca.gov.

Future Community Meetings – To be scheduled

The City anticipates hosting additional community meetings to discuss topics such as traffic, site planning, architecture and aesthetics of the project. Please contact Kristi Bascom to sign up to receive notice of future community meetings regarding this project. Notices about future meetings on the project will also be posted on this webpage.

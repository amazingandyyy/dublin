Status

Project Description

The Applicant has submitted a Pre-Application  for a Site Development Review Permit and Tentative Parcel Map(s) to develop 54-acres of the SCS Dublin site (north of Dublin Boulevard).

Background

In 2022, the City Council approved the SCS Dublin project consisting of a  General Plan/Eastern Dublin Specific Plan Amendment,  Planned Development with Stage 1 and Stage 2 Development Plans and Development Agreement. The SCS Dublin project is a mixed-use project consisting of up to 265,000 square feet of commercial uses and 650 residential units and based on the Preferred Plan approved by City Council on February 15, 2022.

The City of Dublin prepared a Draft Environmental Impact Report (DEIR) for the SCS Dublin project and provided a 45- day public review period which ended on September 6, 2022. The DEIR and Final EIR, including responses to all comments received on the DEIR is available under “Documents.”

On November 15, 2022, the City Council held a first reading of the ordinances, certified the EIR and approved the General Plan and Eastern Dublin Specific Plan amendments. On December 6, 2022, the City Council held a second reading and adopted the ordinances.

Current Status

The City Council held a  study session on the project on March 22, 2023 tp discuss the details of the architecture along Finnian Way and the residential neighborhoods, the location of the Public/Semi-Public parcel and associated affordable housing, the location and size of the community event space and the town square amenities. No formal action was taken at the Study Session. The next steps is for the developer to submit a formal application.

The Applicant, Trumark Homes, LLC, filed applications for the  development of 165.5-acre site with a 573-unit residential project within six neighborhoods, two neighborhood parks 11.5 acres, and a two-acre Semi-Public Site. The proposed development includes four architecture styles: Traditional Farmhouse, Modern Farmhouse, California Revival, and Contemporary, as shown in the project plans. The design and use of the Semi-Public site has not been determined.

  • Neighborhood 1 is the northern most area of the development. It includes 101 units and has the largest lots with a minimum lot size of 6,500 square feet. Unit sizes range from 4,414 square feet to 4,883 square feet.
  • Neighborhood 2 is located on the northern end and straddles Croak Road. It includes 98 units with a minimum lot size of 5,225 sf. Unit sizes range from 3,658 square feet to 3,881 square feet.
  • Neighborhood 3 includes 85 units and has minimum lot size of 5,500 square feet. Unit sizes range from 2,377 square feet to 2,683 square feet. This neighborhood also include six Zero Lot Line Single Family Affordable Units.
  • Neighborhood 4 is located on the west side of Croak Road adjacent to the existing Jordan Ranch neighborhood. It includes 85 units with a minimum lot size of 3,960 sf. Unit sizes range from 2,608 square feet to 2,928 square feet.
  • Neighborhood 5 is located on the northern end and straddles Croak Road. It includes 94 units with a minimum lot size of 3,360 sf. Unit sizes range from 1,621 square feet to 1,671 square feet. This neighborhood also include four Zero Lot Line Single Family Affordable Units.
  • Neighborhood 6 is the located on the south end of the property along the extension of Central Parkway adjacent to the southern park.  It includes 100 medium-density rowhomes. Unit sizes range from 1,915 square feet to 2,104 square feet. This neighborhood also includes four affordable units
  • The “Zero Lot Line” product was design specifically for 14 units for affordable housing dispersed throughout the East Ranch Development
